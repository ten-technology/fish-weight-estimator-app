1. Information the App Collects

a. User-Provided Information

The App allows users to input:

  • Length of fish (in inches, centimeters, or other units)
  • Girth of fish (in inches, centimeters, or other units)
  • Type of fish (selected from predefined fish categories)

This information is processed locally on your device to estimate the weight of the fish. No user-inputted data is sent to external servers, stored, or shared with third parties.

b. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect non-personal data automatically to improve the App's performance and functionality:

  • App usage information: This includes anonymized crash reports, feature interaction statistics, and error logs collected via third-party analytics tools, such as Firebase or similar, to improve the App's functionality.
  • Device information: Includes non-identifiable technical data such as device type, operating system version, and language settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

The input provided by users (length, girth, and fish type) is used solely for calculating fish weight estimates. This information is processed locally on your device to deliver the result in-app.

Anonymized and aggregated app usage data (if collected) may be used for the following purposes:

  • Troubleshooting technical issues and crashes
  • Understanding user behavior to improve app features and usability
  • Analytics and performance improvement

We do not sell, lease, or share any data collected with third parties for marketing or other purposes.
