Fish Weight Calculator

General Formula

Weight (lbs) = (Girth² × Length) / Factor

  • • Girth: Circumference at widest point (inches)
  • • Length: Total length (inches)
  • • Factor: Species-specific constant

Species-Specific Factors

Freshwater Species

  • • Bass: 1200
  • • Pike: 800
  • • Trout: 800
  • • Walleye: 900
  • • Sunfish: 1200
  • • Gar: 1050

Saltwater Species

  • • Salmon: 800
  • • Redfish: 900
  • • Snook: 800
  • • Bluefish: 1200
  • • Bonefish: 1100
  • • Mackerel: 1300

Important Notes

  • 1. All measurements must be in inches
  • 2. Measure girth at the widest point of the fish
  • 3. Length should be total length (tip of snout to tip of tail)
  • 4. Results are estimates; actual weights may vary
  • 5. Formulas assume average fish condition
